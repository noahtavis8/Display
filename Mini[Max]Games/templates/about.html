Welcome, one and all, to

!

Created by Noah Tavis, this Flask-based web application is

inspired by the course material of Harvard's CS50 and CS50AI!

Each game is accompanied by its own bot, trained specifically to

tackle the unique logic of the game its tailored to.

In fact, the name of this web app is based on the popular MiniMax algorithm,

in which the computer looks at various possible future game

states and decides the best way forward. Both the Tic-Tac-Toe and Connect 4

AI's use MiniMax as their decision engines!

This combination of programs will serve as my submission for CS50's

final project. If learning more about computer programming sounds

interesting to you, I'd highly recommend checking them out at Edx.

The course is free and it covers a wide range of programming basics, from C to SQL to Python.

Thank you for visiting!

I hope you find playing these simple games as interesting as I found making them.
